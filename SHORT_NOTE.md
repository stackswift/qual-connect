# Design Choices: Supplier Query Module (Smart Food Safe Solutions Inc)

## Why these screens?
- The screens are designed for clarity and minimalism, allowing QA managers to quickly select suppliers, submit queries, and track status without confusion or clutter.
- Status indicators and card/timeline views make it easy to visualize query progress and supplier responsiveness.
- Consistent layout, clear labels, and accessible forms ensure usability for all team members.

## How is it simple for the user?
- The flow reduces steps: select supplier → submit query → view status, minimizing time spent and errors.
- Search and filter features help users find suppliers instantly.
- Popups and feedback guide users through each action.

## Technology & Domain Expertise
- The solution is designed for Smart Food Safe Solutions Inc, targeting global small to medium-scale food processing industries.
- The architecture is ready for integration with smart technologies: blockchain (for audit trails), IoT (for real-time compliance), cloud (for scalability), and AI/ML (for query analytics and automation).
- The UI/UX is prototyped in Figma/Adobe XD, with a focus on strong communication, conceptual thinking, and typography.

## Ownership & Delivery
- All deliverables are documented, versioned, and delivered on time, with clear communication and design rationale.
- The style guide and design system ensure future scalability and consistency.
